OUR MISSION

Our product ensures that hoosiers have every opportunity to help each other in their communities. By having access to the closest food banks near you, you will be able to access to donate excess food to a food bank near you. Due to COVID-19, many hoosiers are struggling with food insecurity, and having this will allow others to easily help people in their own communities.
